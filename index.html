About Me

A little about me

I'm a final-year B.Tech student in Computer Science and Engineering at NMAM Institute of Technology, Nitte, with a focus on Data Science and Analytics. My passion lies in extracting meaning from messy datasets and turning them into clear, actionable outcomes.

During my internship at Zidio Development, I built dashboards on top of IPL match data and developed an NLP-powered employee mood analyzer — both projects that live at the intersection of technical craft and real-world utility.

Outside of data work, I enjoy building web applications and exploring how machine learning models can be deployed as accessible tools — not just notebooks.
